🦁 The Lion and the Mouse

One day a lion was sleeping in the forest. A little mouse ran over his paw and woke him up. The lion caught the mouse. The mouse begged, “Please let me go. One day I may help you.” The lion laughed but let the mouse go. Later the lion was caught in a hunter’s net. The mouse came and chewed the ropes and freed the lion.

Moral: Kindness is never wasted.
