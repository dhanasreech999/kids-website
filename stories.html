📖 Kids Stories

The Honest Woodcutter

A woodcutter dropped his axe in the river. A god appeared and gave him a golden axe, but he said it was not his. Because of his honesty, the god rewarded him with all the axes.

Moral: Honesty is the best policy.

The Lion and the Mouse

A small mouse once helped a mighty lion.

The Thirsty Crow

A thirsty crow found a pot with little water. It dropped stones into the pot until the water rose high enough to drink.

Moral: Intelligence solves problems.

The Hare and the Tortoise

A fast hare laughed at a slow tortoise. In a race, the hare slept while the tortoise kept walking and won the race.

Moral: Slow and steady wins the race.

The Greedy Dog

A dog carrying a bone saw his reflection in water. Thinking it was another dog with a bigger bone, he barked and lost his own bone.

Moral: Greed leads to loss.
